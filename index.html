Cleaner Streets

Creating Cleaner neighborhoods

Cleaner Streets revitalizes communities by creating cleaner, healthier, and safer neighborhoods. By providing cleaner neighborhoods, we create a better environment for all. So join us as we begin our journey.

Our Mission

We’re tranforming communities by creating cleaner, healthier communities one neighborhood at a time. Cleaner Streets empowers residents through compensated cleanups and reinvestment into their community, creating a better place to live, work, and play.

Our Service

Cleaner Streets provide a way for people to earn money by simply cleaning up neighborhoods, creating more cleaner and nicer living conditions, people can access this via website or application.

How does it work?

We will have organizers or managers who will facilitate people who have signed up, keeping a log of names and allowing for pay. You just sign up, look for any available or upcoming cleaning days and a time and location will be provided.

Thread Cycling

Our Threadcycling process transforms waste into fabric


• Our company collects waste
• The waste is transferred to a recycling facility
• The waste is seperated and broken down
• The waste that was broken down is turned into polyester
• That polyester is then used to create new clothing
