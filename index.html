Who are we?

Cleaner Streets is a non-profit organization that aims to help keep communities clean. By deploying autonomous AI robots that detect and map trash in communities in real-time, we can quickly and efficiently remove trash within communities.

Our Robots

CS-BOTS are fully autonomous robots designed to scan and map trash accumulation within communities. They are equipped with advanced technology that allows them to navigate all terrains, collect trash, and deposit it at designated drop-off points.

A Simple, Powerful Process

Our system makes community cleaning intelligent and efficient

1
Scan & Detect

Our robots autonomously patrol urban areas using advanced computer vision to identify and classify litter.

2
Track & Map

All data is sent to our cloud platform, creating real-time maps of litter hotspots for targeted cleaning.

3
Collect & Recycle

The robot collects the trash, sorts it, and brings it back to base for responsible recycling and disposal.

4
Engage & Reward

Through our app, communities can track progress, report problem areas, and earn rewards for their involvement.
